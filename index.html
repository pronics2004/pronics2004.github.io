About Me

I am a researcher at IBM Research AI and my broad research interests include machine learning, deep learning and signal processing.

My current work focuses on exploring structure in the data using generative models and developing algorithms that are data efficient. My research also involves developing theory and practical systems for impactful machine learning applications that demand constraints such as robustness, fairness and interpretability.
